Starter Kit is your gate the BigClown's world. You will learn, how to set up Kit, connect it with computer and send notifications to you mobile devices with pushing button on kit.

1Build Hardware

Put all three modules together to build the Push Button. Note the orientation of the Mini Battery Module and put the batteries in.

2Set-Up Playground

For managin devices, functions we will use BigClown playground. It helps you connect everything together.

  1. Download and Install BigClown Playground application
  2. Plug Radio Dongle to your computer
  3. Open BigClown Playground and open "Devices" tab
  4. Click on "Connect" button

3Set-Up Blynk App

For receiving notification on mobile device we will use Blynk Application.

  1. Download the Blynk app from App Store or Google Play. Create an account and log-in.
  2. Click on "QR code" icon in right corner and scan our QR Code
  3. Scan QR Code
  4. Send Auth Token to your e-mail

4Connect BigClown and Blynk

The final step is to connect Node-RED and Blynk together, so you can get the notifications.

  1. Open "Functions" tab in BigClown Playground
  2. Double click on Blynk notification node
  3. Click on "Pencil" icon and paste Auth Token from Blynk
  4. Save changes by clicking "Done"
  5. Click on "Deploy" button

5Thats all!

Your project is done, you can Push the Button!
